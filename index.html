I am an Applied Scientist in AMXCS at Amazon, where I design and deploy NLP and generative AI systems at enterprise scale. My work bridges research and production: I develop novel methods in topic modeling, LLM hallucination detection, and fact verification, then ship them as systems serving 170K+ daily responses and generating over $540K in cost savings per quarter. My research spans topic modeling (S2VNTM, KDSTM, PLTK++), LLM safety (hallucination detection and correction), mechanistic interpretability (LOLAMEME), and multi-document summarization (JADS).

I hold a granted U.S. patent in computer vision-based safety detection, with an additional patent filed covering LLM-driven topic modeling. Before Amazon, I completed my MS in Computer Science at NYIT and my BE in Computer Engineering from the University of Pune.
